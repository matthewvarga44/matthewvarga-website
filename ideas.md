## Selected Approach: Modern Professional Minimalism

I will proceed with the **Modern Professional Minimalism** approach. This style aligns best with the reference site (Coach Carson) while establishing a unique, professional identity for Matthew Varga. It balances the need for authority (crucial for a coach) with approachability.

**Key Design Decisions:**
- **Colors**: Navy Blue primary, Coral Orange accent, White/Light Gray background.
- **Typography**: Montserrat for headings, Open Sans for body.
- **Layout**: Clean, card-based, with asymmetric hero sections.
- **Visuals**: Professional photography, clean icons, subtle geometric patterns.
